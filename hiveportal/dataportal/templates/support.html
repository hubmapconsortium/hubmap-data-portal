FAQ:

How do I access data?

In order to access your courses, simply tap the "Courses" dropdown menu on the navigation bar above. Then, pick which course you want to navigate to.

How do I add a new course?

When you click on the "Courses"option above in the navigation bar, the dropdown menu should display an option for "Add a Course"

How do I access my assignments?

In order to access your assignments, simply tap the "Assignments" dropdown menu on the navigation bar above. Then, pick which assignment you want to navigate to.
